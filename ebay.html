eBay.com
About: eBay is a multinational e-commerce corporation based in San Jose, California that facilitates consumer-to-consumer and business-to-consumer sales through its website. eBay is an online auction and shopping website in which people and businesses buy and sell a wide variety of products and services domestically and internationally. The website is free to use for buyers, but sellers are charged fees for listing items after a limited number of free listings, having different features, and when those items are sold ebay takes a certain percentage of commission.
Reviews/Ratings: eBay allows buyers to rate and review the sellers based on their experience with them by using stars and in the following four categories: Item as described; Shipping Time; Shipping and Handling Charges; Communication. eBay tends to focus a lot more on the reviews and ratings of the seller then the product itself. Reviews are associated with a specific product, ensuring a reliable and consistent user experience for buyers.
